Determining the purpose of the investigation is the most crucial step, which is the basis of setting the question and setting the object of the investigation. Determining the purpose of the investigation can ensure the direction of the investigation is correct. Identifying the investigator can narrow the scope of the investigation and obtain more effective information. Survey of people in different positions and identities can obtain the results of the survey from various aspects to ensure that the results of the survey are not one-sided and only beneficial to some groups.
